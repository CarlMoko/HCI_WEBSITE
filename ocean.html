Ocean Adventure Subic Bay

Ocean Adventure Subic Bay is an eco-friendly marine park located in Subic Bay Freeport Zone, Philippines. It offers a unique and educational experience for visitors to interact with marine animals, learn about conservation, and enjoy various water activities. The park is dedicated to the preservation and protection of marine life and habitat, providing a fun and informative experience for all ages. Visitors can enjoy shows, encounters with dolphins and sea lions, as well as snorkeling and diving opportunities. Ocean Adventure SBMA is a must-visit destination for those who love the ocean and want to learn more about marine conservation.

About

Ocean Adventure is a marine park located in Subic Bay, Philippines, offering interactive and educational
experiences with marine animals. The park was established in 2001 by Subic Bay Marine Exploratorium, Inc. (SBMEI), a joint
venture between the Government of the Philippines and private investors.
Since its inception, Ocean Adventure has been dedicated to promoting marine conservation and responsible tourism through
its various programs and activities. The park is home to a wide variety of marine animals, including dolphins, sea lions, sharks,
and various species of fish and reptiles.
Visitors to Ocean Adventure can enjoy a range of activities, such as dolphin and sea lion shows, educational talks and feeding
sessions, and interactive encounters with the animals. The park also offers opportunities for guests to participate in conservation
and research efforts, such as the adoption program for dolphins and sea lions.
Ocean Adventure has become a popular destination for tourists and locals alike, attracting thousands of visitors each year.
The park continues to be a leader in marine conservation and education in the Philippines, inspiring visitors to appreciate and
protect the wonders of the ocean.
